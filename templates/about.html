About Us

The Health Prediction System is an advanced ML-powered platform designed to help users understand their possible health conditions based on symptoms they enter.

Our system uses modern AI-driven prediction models to provide early insights and guide users toward better health awareness.

Our Vision

To make early health awareness accessible to everyone using intelligent machine learning.

Our Technology

Built using Python, Flask, ML Models (Random Forest, SVM, Logistic Regression), HTML, CSS.

Our Goal

Helping individuals identify possible diseases early and take informed health decisions.
